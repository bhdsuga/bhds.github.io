Biomedical & Health Data Science and AI Seminars

The UGA Biomedical & Health Data Science and AI Seminars are monthly online seminars that cover interdisciplinary research topics in biomedical image analysis, biomedical informatics, public health, statistics, engineering, data science (DS), and artificial intelligence (AI). We aim to bring together researchers from these fields to discuss exciting topics at the intersection of DS/AI and biomedical/health domains.
